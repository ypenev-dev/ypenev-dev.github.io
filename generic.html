Living, learning, & leveling up one day at a time.

I don’t describe myself by the work I've done. I portray myself by the work I have to do. Capacities can be instructed, character can growth. I like to keep learning, continue testing myself, and do interesting things that matter.

Fueled by energy and enthusiasm, I’m easily willing to follow my fascinations wherever they take me. I’m passionate, expressive,with a ability to entertain and inspire. I’m never satisfied to just come up with ideas. Instead I act on them.

My energy fuels me in my interests, I’m a fast learner, able to pick up new skills and juggle different projects and roles with relative ease. I like to develop expertise in a number of areas over the course of my life and career.

I’m a people-person and a natural storyteller. I’m at my best when I’m sharing my creative expressions with others.
